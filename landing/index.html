Features

Everything your game needs.

From finding your first group to running a city-wide tournament — MatchUp has every step covered.

Find Groups Nearby

Discover groups by city and skill level. RSVP, get QR check-in tickets, and play within minutes.

Tournaments & Brackets

Create or join tournaments. Brackets, seeding, results — all live, all in one place.

Live Scoreboards

Real-time score tracking. Share live match links — friends and family can follow from anywhere.

Smart Ticketing

Auto-issued QR tickets on RSVP. Hosts scan to check in. No paper, no hassle.

Share & Invite

Share group links via Zalo, Messenger, or any platform. One tap to bring your friends in.

Health Hub

Track fitness, session history, and wellness alongside your game performance.
