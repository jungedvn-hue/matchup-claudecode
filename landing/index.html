Features

Everything your game needs

From finding your first group to running a city-wide tournament — MatchUp has you covered.

🗺️
Find Groups Nearby

Discover pickleball groups by city and skill level. Join sessions, RSVP, and get QR check-in tickets automatically.

🏆
Tournaments & Brackets

Create or join tournaments. Manage brackets, track results, and follow standings — all real-time.

📊
Live Scoreboards

Real-time score tracking. Share live match links with friends and family watching from anywhere.

🎟️
Smart Ticketing

Auto-issued QR tickets on RSVP. Hosts scan to check in players — no paper, no hassle.

📣
Share & Invite

Share group links via Zalo, Messenger, or any platform. One tap to invite your friends.

❤️
Health Hub

Track your fitness stats, session history, and wellness alongside your game performance.
